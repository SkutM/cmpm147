Experiment 2 - Underwater Scene

Description

This experiment uses p5.js to generate a dynamic underwater scene with fish, bubbles, and a procedurally generated ocean floor.

Technical

The scene is rendered in a canvas that resizes with the browser window. Fish are drawn with OOP, and bubbles float with time-based animation. Clicking "New Fish" regenerates the scene with a new random seed.

Reflection

This was a smoother experience after the first experiment. Understanding p5.js integration with my portfolio gave me a stronger grasp of canvas-based animation and DOM interaction.
